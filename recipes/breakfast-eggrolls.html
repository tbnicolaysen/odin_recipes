Breakfast Eggrolls

Ingredients and Equipment

  • Eggroll Wrappers
  • Eggs
  • Sausage
  • Potatoes (Shredded Hash Browns)
  • Cooking oil of your choice (Vegetable)

  • Pan for cooking
  • Clean board
  • Big Bowl!

The Plan

We're going to take the word "egg" in egg rolls far too
seriously here. Its a delicious all in one (or two, or three)
breakfast roll!

The Process

  1. Chop down the sausage. Smaller sausage mixes in better
  2. Get your sausage cooked in the pan. Maybe not
    all the way because you're leaving it in there
    with the eggs afterwards.
  3. Do you know how to cook your eggs? I sure
    hope so, because I don't remember. I can tell
    you that I recommend you scramble them for this
  4. Once the eggs are well prepared you'll want to
    toss everything into that bowl
  5. Get those hash browns crispy! When your hash
    browns are ready, toss them in the bowl too
  6. Mix up the ingredients a bit! Every egg roll should have
    plenty of everything inside!
  7. Place egg rolls on the clean board and wrap up the mix
    one after another in the wrappers.
  8. Clean out the pan and heat some oil at the bottom
  9. Once the oil is shimmering place the Egg rolls in and
    let them fry. You'll want them browned all over!
  10. Once they're all done, you're good to go!
